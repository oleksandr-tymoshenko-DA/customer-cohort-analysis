## Google Sheets (Validation)

Validation spreadsheet used to cross-check SQL results and ensure correctness of cohort calculations.

The sheet was used to verify:
- cohort sizes
- retention rates
- ARPU (Average Revenue Per User)
- LTV (Lifetime Value)
